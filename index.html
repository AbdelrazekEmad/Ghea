Alzheimer's Care

We provide our services to people living with Alzheimer's to help them go about their lives easier and do their daily needs and help them feel independent to ensure that the quality of life provided to them is greatly improved

Care for all ages

Our care is not limited to the elderly or children. We provide home medical care for all ages and conditions, whether they are in need or have other health conditions requiring home care.

Care for the elderly

Older people in health care's neediest age groups help them go about their lives quickly. We spend our time creating a plan tailored to the requirements and objectives of the elderly who receive care.

Childcare

Children need special care that gives them good health care, comfort, and safety, whether the child has a disability, illness, or other problem that requires constant and regular care and attention. Your first choice is for medical services.

Post-surgical care

After performing surgical operations, specifically in the convalescent period, the patient has difficulty moving and doing his daily tasks efficiently and needs to take medical real estate at specific times, so we offer post-operative care service with specialists from GHEA

Antenatal and post-natal care

Prenatal and postpartum need great qualifications for some ladies in this period with a lot of problems and difficulties that need special care, and this is what we offer you in GHEA

Short Term Care

Most cases require short-term care or a certain period to perform daily tasks, whether for the elderly, children, or other ages and health conditions.

Personal Care

We understand each individual's circumstances and develop a plan that best suits their needs. According to this plan, caregivers will be keen to care for them with kindness.

Personal Care Escort

Chronic diseases or short-term mobility problems can lead to loneliness and social isolation in patients. This difficult period requires a companion that can bring a sense of humanity and participation so that loneliness does not affect their health and well-being.
